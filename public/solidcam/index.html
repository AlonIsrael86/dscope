Lead generation, engineered

Why the Dscope widget wins SolidCAM more leads.

It is not just a chatbot - it is a popup built to convert. Here is what does the work.

A popup that opens at the perfect moment

Smart triggers bring the widget forward exactly when it matters - after a few seconds, at a scroll depth, on exit intent, or only on the right page.

→More leads: you catch the visitor at peak interest instead of hoping they hunt down a contact form.

Messages that rewrite themselves

The widget reads the page and changes the words on the button to match what the visitor is looking at right now.

→More leads: the right message at the right moment grabs attention and pulls far more clicks than a static bubble.

Custom design in SolidCAM's exact brand

Not a generic third-party bot - the widget is built in your colors, your logo and your tone, so it feels native to your site.

→More leads: visitors click what they trust, and an on-brand widget reads as SolidCAM's own - trust converts.

Chat and voice in one widget

Visitors type or talk out loud, get instant accurate answers about iMachining, integrations and licensing - and can even share their screen.

→More leads: nobody bounces to a competitor for an answer - every high-intent visitor is engaged and captured.

Every chat becomes a scored lead in your CRM

Name, phone and email - plus an AI lead-quality score and summary - pushed to your CRM the moment a conversation ends.

→More leads: sales works the hottest leads within minutes, and speed-to-lead is the number one driver of won B2B deals.

Smart forms that qualify and route

Multi-step forms that branch by industry, role or machine type and segment every lead automatically.

→More leads: you capture qualified, routed leads with far higher completion than one long generic form.
