Every feature - and why it drives leads.

The complete toolkit, mapped to one goal: more qualified demo requests and trial sign-ups.

AI Sales Agent (chat)

Trained on your own knowledge base - iMachining, SolidWorks/Inventor integration, post-processors, licensing. It answers technical questions accurately and instantly, in the visitor's language.

→Why it brings leads: engineers get the answer that unblocks their evaluation immediately, so they stay on your site and move toward a demo instead of bouncing to a competitor.

Real-time Voice Agent

Visitors can talk to the agent out loud - and even share their screen or webcam so the agent can see their CAD setup and guide them live.

→Why it brings leads: a spoken conversation feels like a real sales call with zero scheduling friction - it captures high-intent prospects and qualifies them on the spot.

Smart Lead Forms (branching)

Multi-step forms that branch by the visitor's profile - industry, role, machine type, or need (aerospace vs. job shop) - each on its own clean screen.

→Why it brings leads: the form qualifies and segments automatically, asks only what's relevant, and completes far more often than one long generic form.

Smart Triggers

The widget opens itself at the right moment: after a set time, at a scroll depth, on exit intent, or only on specific URLs - and its message changes per section (see the live trigger in the bottom-left corner).

→Why it brings leads: it engages the visitor precisely when interest peaks, with a message matched to the page - right offer, right time, more conversions.

Custom Popups & Widgets

Brand-matched popups designed for SolidCAM - "Book a live demo", "Download free trial", "Talk to an application engineer" - styled to look native to your site.

→Why it brings leads: a contextual, on-brand call-to-action converts dramatically better than a buried contact link or a generic chat bubble.

Instant Lead Capture → your CRM

Name, phone and email are captured with fixed, reliable keys and pushed in real time to your CRM, Google Sheets, or any tool via webhook.

→Why it brings leads: no lead sits unseen in an inbox - sales is notified and can follow up while the prospect is still on the page.

Branded Thank-You Experience

After submitting, the visitor lands on a custom confirmation screen with next steps - a calendar link, a trial download, or curated resources.

→Why it brings leads: it keeps momentum right after the conversion, sets clear expectations, and reduces no-shows on the demos you book.

Post-Conversation Intelligence

After every chat or call, the AI extracts a summary, the visitor's intent, and a lead-quality score - automatically attached to the lead.

→Why it brings leads: sales works the hottest leads first with full context, no manual triage - higher contact rates and a higher close rate.

Filterable Conversation Archive

Every chat and voice session is stored and searchable - filter by lead score, intent, or topic, and download voice recordings.

→Why it brings leads: you see exactly what prospects ask before buying, sharpen your messaging, and surface buying signals you'd otherwise miss.

Live Tools & Actions

The agent can call your systems mid-conversation - check the nearest reseller by region, book a demo slot, or send a trial license link, all without leaving the chat.

→Why it brings leads: it removes every step between interest and action - the meeting gets booked inside the conversation, not "later".

Multilingual

One agent speaks your prospects' languages - serving SolidCAM's global base of resellers and manufacturers worldwide.

→Why it brings leads: people convert in their native language - you capture qualified leads across every region, not just English-speaking ones.

Full Brand Customization

Colors, logo, avatar and tone of voice are tailored to SolidCAM, so the agent feels like part of your site - not a third-party bot.

→Why it brings leads: trust drives action - visitors engage far more with an on-brand experience they recognize as SolidCAM's own.

Integrations & Automations

Connect leads to email sequences, Slack alerts, CRM stages and any workflow via n8n / webhooks - fully automated routing and nurturing.

→Why it brings leads: instant routing means the right rep responds in minutes - and speed-to-lead is the single biggest driver of won B2B deals.
